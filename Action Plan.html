        My action plan is to give suggestions on how we can make our city sustainable using statistics and probability to the GMDA
   (Gurugram Metropolitan Development Authority).

   I would suggest them to use the concept of statistics and probability to study weather patterns around the city and make
   a plan to make implants of green energy around the city.The green energy used would depend on the weather patterns.

   I would also suggest them to study traffic patterns in a few parts of the city and to make a plan of the duration of the
   green/red light on traffic lights.They could also study if this has any effect on the pollution levels.
